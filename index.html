Product Descriptions With Photos

Blue Pens

Blue pens are classic. Everybody loves them and uses them. You can go right with a trusty blue pen. This blue pen is 100% manufactured by me and not anybody else. The pen doesn’t smear ink anywhere. The pen is built like a cylinder and it’s kind of an awkward shape to hold and write with. Sometimes it can slip out of your hands. Ink lasts for what seems like forever. Very smooth writing. Overall, very nice pen.

Black Pens

Black pens are also a classic. Many people prefer color though, but that’s not a necessity. The black pens work just as good. These ones specifically write very smooth and flow like water out the pen. Because it’s so smooth, sometimes the ink will smudge on the paper or on your hand and it can run out fast. There’s a small grip area for your finger so that they don’t slip. Overall, very nice pen to write fast with.

Red Pens

Nobody uses red pens anymore. The last time I remember somebody using them was my teacher when she marked about 3/4th’s of my math exam that I failed. But you know what can get you into red pens? This one specifically! The ink runs smoothly just like the black pens and it also has a grip area for your finger so that they don’t slip. The grip even has lines too so it’s fancier. Overall, very underrated product.
